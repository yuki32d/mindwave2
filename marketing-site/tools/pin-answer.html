How It Works

Visual location-based assessment

Interactive Image Pinning

Students click on images to mark correct locations

1. Upload Your Image

Upload a map, diagram, anatomy chart, or any image. Ask "Where is the heart?" or "Locate Paris on the map."

2. Students Click to Pin

Students click on the image to place a pin at the location they think is correct.

3. Auto-Grade by Proximity

Answers are graded based on how close the pin is to the correct location - set tolerance levels.
