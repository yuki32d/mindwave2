Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MindWave

How does the subscription work?

MindWave offers flexible monthly or annual subscriptions. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time through your dashboard. We use Stripe for secure payment processing, and all plans include a 14-day free trial.

Is my student data secure?

Absolutely. We are SOC2 compliant and follow FERPA/GDPR guidelines to ensure student data is always encrypted and protected. Your data is stored in secure, isolated databases with regular backups.

Can I export student data and reports?

Yes! You can export comprehensive reports in CSV, PDF, or Excel formats. We also offer native integrations with popular LMS platforms like Canvas, Blackboard, and Moodle.

What kind of support do you offer?

All plans include email support and access to our comprehensive documentation. Team plans get priority support with faster response times. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated account manager and 24/7 phone support.

How does the AI quiz builder work?

Simply describe your topic in natural language, and our AI analyzes your input to generate relevant questions, answer options, and explanations. You can customize difficulty levels, question types, and regenerate as needed.

Can I use MindWave with my existing LMS?

Yes! MindWave integrates seamlessly with Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, Google Classroom, and Microsoft Teams. Grades sync automatically, and students can access quizzes through your LMS.
