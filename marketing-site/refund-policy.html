Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

At MindWave, we want you to be completely satisfied with our service. This policy outlines our refund and cancellation terms.

1. Free Trial

We offer a free trial period for new users. During the trial:

  • You can explore all features without charge
  • You can cancel anytime before the trial ends
  • No payment is required until the trial period expires
  • You will receive a reminder before being charged

2. Subscription Cancellation

How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time through:

  • Your account settings dashboard
  • Contacting our support team at support@mindwave.app

Effect of Cancellation

  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period
  • You will retain access to all features until the subscription expires
  • No further charges will be made after cancellation
  • You can reactivate your subscription at any time

3. Refund Policy

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a full refund within 30 days of your initial purchase if you're not satisfied with MindWave.

Eligibility for Refunds

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • You request a refund within 30 days of your first payment
  • You experience technical issues that prevent platform use
  • We are unable to deliver the promised service
  • You were charged incorrectly or fraudulently

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are NOT available for:

  • Subscription renewals after the initial 30-day period
  • Partial months of service
  • Accounts suspended for Terms of Service violations
  • Change of mind after 30 days

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Email us at refunds@mindwave.app
  2. Include your account email and reason for refund
  3. Provide your order/transaction ID
  4. We will process your request within 5-7 business days

5. Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • It may take 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account
  • You will receive an email confirmation once processed

6. Subscription Downgrades

If you downgrade your subscription plan:

  • The change takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle
  • You will not receive a prorated refund for the difference
  • You retain access to higher-tier features until the downgrade takes effect

7. Annual Subscriptions

For annual subscriptions:

  • The 30-day money-back guarantee applies
  • After 30 days, annual subscriptions are non-refundable
  • You can cancel to prevent auto-renewal for the next year
  • Partial refunds are not provided for unused months

8. Enterprise Plans

Enterprise plan refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact your account manager or email enterprise@mindwave.app for assistance.

9. Payment Disputes

If you have a payment dispute:

  • Contact us first at support@mindwave.app
  • We will work to resolve the issue promptly
  • Chargebacks may result in account suspension
  • We prefer to resolve disputes directly before involving payment processors

10. Service Credits

In cases where a refund is not applicable, we may offer:

  • Service credits for future use
  • Extended subscription period
  • Upgrade to a higher plan at no additional cost

11. Contact Us

For questions about refunds or cancellations:

  • Email: refunds@mindwave.app
  • Support: support@mindwave.app
  • Phone: [Your Support Number]

We're committed to your satisfaction and will work with you to find a fair solution.
