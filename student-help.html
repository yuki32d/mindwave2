‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play games?
Go to the Games section from the sidebar, click on any game card, then click the "Play" button.
Where can I see my scores?
Check the Leaderboard section to see your ranking and compare with classmates.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your campus email to receive a reset code.
Can I access course materials?
Yes! Go to the Courses section to view notes, PDFs, and other materials uploaded by your instructors.

üîß Troubleshooting

Game not loading?
Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R). If the issue persists, clear your browser cache.
Can't see new games?
Make sure the game is published by your instructor. Refresh the page to see the latest updates.
Login issues?
Ensure you're using your campus email (.mca25@cmrit.ac.in for students). Contact support if you still can't login.
Score not updating?
Scores update immediately after game submission. If you don't see your score, try logging out and back in.
