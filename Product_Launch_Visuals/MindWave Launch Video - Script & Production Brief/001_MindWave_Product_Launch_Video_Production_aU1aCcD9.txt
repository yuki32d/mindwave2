## CONTINUITY NOTES

- **Logo presence:** Brain icon appears in Beat 4 (hero reveal), Beat 13 (tagline moment), and Beat 14 (CTA card). Keep consistent scale relationships.
- **Typography system:** One typeface family throughout — rounded sans-serif (e.g., Nunito, Poppins, or similar). Weights: Black for headlines, Medium for body.
- **Transition language:** Use geometric shapes (thin lines, circles, grids) as wipe/reveal devices between every beat — never a plain cut except where impact is intentional (Beat 3 slash).
- **Audio direction:** Punchy electronic underscore — builds from sparse in Beat 1, peaks at Beat 4 (logo reveal), drives through features, swells at Beat 13, resolves cleanly at Beat 14.
- **Pacing rule:** Each beat = exactly 5 seconds. No beat should feel rushed or padded — write voiceover to fit naturally within 5 seconds at a moderate pace.
