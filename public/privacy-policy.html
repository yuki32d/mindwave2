Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MindWave ("we", "our", or "us"). MindWave is an educational platform designed for use by students and faculty within academic institutions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services at mindwave-edu.tech.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and password (stored encrypted).
  • Profile Information: Department, batch, section, roll number, and profile photo.
  • Usage Data: Game scores, quiz submissions, course progress, and platform activity logs.
  • Google Account Data: If you sign in with Google or connect Google Classroom, we access your name, email, and Google Classroom course data based on permissions you grant.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the MindWave platform experience.
  • To track academic progress and generate analytics for faculty.
  • To send important notifications about courses, assignments, and announcements.
  • To sync with Google Classroom for assignments and course materials.
  • To improve platform features and fix technical issues.

4. Google API Services

MindWave's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We only request the minimum Google permissions necessary to operate the platform's classroom integration features. We do not sell or share your Google data with third parties.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Data may be shared with:

  • Faculty and administrators within your institution for academic purposes.
  • Third-party service providers (e.g., MongoDB Atlas for database hosting) under strict data processing agreements.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including password hashing (bcrypt), HTTPS encryption via Cloudflare SSL, and JWT-based authentication tokens. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Inactive accounts may be automatically deactivated after the academic year ends.
